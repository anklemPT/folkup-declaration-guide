Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle any data we collect

Privacy at a Glance

We collect very little data. This site works without requiring personal information. Any analytics data is anonymous and optional — you control whether we collect it through our cookie consent system.

Who we are

Special protection for under-16s

Under European law (GDPR Article 8), young people under 16 have special protection. If you're under 16:

  • Ask a grown-up: Please involve a parent or guardian in privacy decisions
  • Extra protections: We take additional care with any data from young users
  • Educational purpose: This site is designed to help young people learn about digital rights
  • No profiling: We never build behavioral profiles of individual users

What we don't do

  • No personal accounts: You don't need to register or log in
  • No email collection: We don't collect email addresses
  • No social media tracking: No Facebook pixels, Twitter tracking, or similar
  • No cross-site tracking: Analytics only work on this site
  • No data sales: We never sell, rent, or share data with third parties
  • No advertising networks: No Google Ads, ad networks, or behavioral advertising
  • No precise location: We don't track your exact location
  • No device fingerprinting: We don't create unique identifiers for your device

Your rights (GDPR)

Under European data protection law, you have several rights:

Access and portability

  • Right to know: Ask what data we have about you (though it's very little)
  • Data portability: Get a copy of your data in a readable format

Correction and deletion

  • Right to correct: Fix any incorrect data
  • Right to delete: Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict: Limit how we use your data

Consent and objection

  • Withdraw consent: Change your mind about analytics anytime
  • Object to processing: Ask us to stop using your data
  • No automated decisions: We don't make automated decisions about you

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@folkup.app

Technical security

  • HTTPS encryption: All data transmitted securely
  • Minimal data storage: We store as little as possible
  • Regular security updates: Server and software kept up to date
  • Access controls: Only authorized personnel can access systems
  • Data retention limits: Automatic deletion after retention periods
