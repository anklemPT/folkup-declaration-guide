Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies and your privacy choices

Cookie Summary

We use minimal cookies. This site uses only essential cookies for basic functionality and optional analytics cookies with your explicit consent. You have full control over non-essential cookies and can change your preferences anytime.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience. We use cookies responsibly and transparently.

Cookie types we use

Essential cookies (always active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
lang_preference Remember your selected language (EN/RU/PT) Session only Functional
cookie_consent Remember your cookie preferences 1 year Consent
form_progress Save form progress to prevent data loss Session only Functional

Analytics cookies (optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. They collect anonymous data and require your consent.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Data Collected
_analytics_session Track unique visitors anonymously 24 hours Anonymous session ID
_analytics_pageview Understand page popularity 13 months Page URLs, timestamps
_analytics_country Language usage patterns 13 months Country code only

Third-party services

FormSubmit.co (form processing)

When you sign the Declaration, your form is processed through FormSubmit.co. They may set their own cookies:

  • Purpose: Form submission and anti-spam protection
  • Duration: According to FormSubmit.co's policy
  • Privacy: FormSubmit Privacy Policy
  • Control: Required for form functionality, no opt-out available

Self-hosted analytics

Our analytics system (analytics.folkup.app) is:

  • Self-hosted: No data shared with third parties
  • Privacy-focused: No personal data collected
  • GDPR compliant: Anonymous and aggregated data only
  • EU hosted: All data stays within European Union

Special protection for young users

If you're under 16 years old:

  • Ask for help: Please involve a parent or guardian in privacy decisions
  • Enhanced protection: We apply stricter privacy protections for young users
  • Limited data: We collect even less information from users who indicate they're under 16
  • Parental rights: Parents can request information about or deletion of their child's data

Managing cookies in your browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

Browser controls

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

What happens if you disable cookies

  • Essential cookies disabled: Site may not function properly
  • Analytics cookies disabled: No impact on functionality
  • Language preference: You'll need to select language each visit
  • Form progress: Forms won't save progress if you navigate away

Your rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have rights regarding cookies and tracking:

  • Consent withdrawal: Change your mind about analytics cookies anytime
  • Access: Request information about data collected via cookies
  • Deletion: Request deletion of data collected through cookies
  • Objection: Object to processing of personal data
