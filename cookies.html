Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and your rights regarding them

Quick Summary

We use minimal cookies to understand how people use this site. You're in control — you can accept, decline, or withdraw consent anytime. If you're under 16, please ask a grown-up to help you decide.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device. They help websites remember things like your preferences or understand how people use the site.

How we use cookies

This site uses cookies for two main purposes:

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

  • Language preference: Remembers which language you selected (English, Russian, or Portuguese)
  • Consent status: Remembers your choice about analytics cookies

These cookies are necessary for the site to work properly and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

  • Usage statistics: Anonymous data about which pages are visited and how long people stay
  • Popular content: Helps us understand what information is most useful
  • Technical issues: Identifies if anything isn't working properly

We only collect this data if you give permission. No personal information is collected — just general patterns about how the site is used.

Your rights and choices

Under 16?

If you're under 16, please ask a parent or guardian to help you understand this policy and make decisions about cookies. This follows European data protection rules that give special protection to young people.

Managing your consent

You can change your mind about analytics cookies anytime:

Current consent status: Checking...

This will remove your consent and show the consent banner again

Browser controls

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may break some site features)
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set warnings before cookies are stored

What we don't do

  • No tracking across websites: Our analytics only work on this site
  • No personal profiles: We don't build profiles of individual users
  • No selling data: Analytics data is never shared with advertisers or third parties
  • No location tracking: We don't collect precise location information

Data controller

This site is operated by FolkUp. For questions about cookies or data protection, contact:

  • Email: privacy@folkup.app
  • More information: Privacy Policy

Updates to this policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
